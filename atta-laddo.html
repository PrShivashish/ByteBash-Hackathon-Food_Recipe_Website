Atta Laddoo

About Atta Laddoo

Atta Laddoo is a popular North Indian dessert recipe that can be made for festivals and special ocassions. This atta laddoo recipe can be made with just three simple ingredients: wheat flour, ghee and cane sugar (jaggery). You can also add some dry fruits to accentuate the taste of these laddoos. These delicious laddoos can be enjoyed anytime, whenever you are hungry because it is filling as well as light on the stomach. This delectable sweet dish is a fusion of jaggery and refined flour. What makes this delicacy even more delicious is the flavour of ghee roasted Atta amalgamated with jaggery, which gives this laddoo a balanced taste.

Ingredients

  • 500 gm wheat flour
  • 500 gm powdered jaggery
  • 60 gm ghee

Procedure

Step-1 : Roast the flour until brown in colour

Step-2 : Mix jaggery with flour and make laddoos when the mixture is still warm
Mix powdered jaggery with this roasted flour. Next, add ghee to this mixture and stir. When your mixture is thick enough, let it cool down a bit. Now make small balls (laddoos) using the mixture. Your laddoos are ready now. Store them in an airtight container and relish them any time.

Tips

  • Add dry fruits of your choice to the laddoos to make them richer.
  • Make sure you roast the flour till brown in colour, otherwise the laddoos won't taste as good.
