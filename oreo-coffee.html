Oreo Coffee Milk Shake

If you are looking for a shake that has the goodness of shake as well as cold coffee in it, then you should give this milkshake a try! Oreo Coffee Milk Shake is an amazingly delicious shake recipe that you can prepare in no time. This beverage recipe will be loved by people of all age groups and can be enjoyed in breakfast as well as in evening. Well, all you need is: Coffee powder, cold milk, Oreo cookies, vanilla ice cream, chocolate syrup and sugar along with some ice cubes. So, do try this delicious shake for your loved ones and enjoy with them.

Ingredients for Oreo Coffee Shake

  1. 2 cup cold milk
  2. 2 tablespoon sugar
  3. 1 tablespoon chocolate syrup
  4. 8 ice cubes
  5. 3 teaspoon coffee powder
  6. 10 oreo cookies
  7. 4 scoops vanilla ice cream

Procedure

  • For this amazing milkshake, all you need is a blender jar to process the yummy drink. To prepare this shake recipe, take 3 Oreo Biscuits and form a powder of it. Keep this Oreo powder aside until required.
  • Now, you have to prepare your glass as chilled. Take a highball glass and apply a little chocolate syrup on the edges and now dip the rim of the glass in Oreo powder. Now, apply some more chocolate syrup on the glass it will form the pattern. Put this glass in the freezer till your shake is ready.
  • Finally, take a blender jar and add cold milk in it along with sugar and coffee powder. Blend well at high speed until its nice and frothy.
  • Then, add the remaining Oreo biscuit in it and 3 scoops of vanilla ice cream along with a few ice cubes. Blend once again nicely.
  • Once done, take out the frozen glass and fill it with the prepared shake. Next, add a scoop of vanilla ice cream and garnish with the prepared Oreo biscuit powder (step 1) and a half Oreo biscuit. Oreo Coffee Milk Shake is now ready, enjoy!
