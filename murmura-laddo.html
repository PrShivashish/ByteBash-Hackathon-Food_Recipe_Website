Kurmura Ladoo

About Kurmura Ladoo

Kurmura Ladoo or Puffed rice ladoo is a toothsome dessert which is highly popular in North India. These delicious ladoos are made by Punjabis specially at the time of Lohri. The easy-to-make ladoos are made with just three simple ingredients which are puffed rice (kurmura), ghee, and cane sugar (jaggery). Apart from festivals, these ladoos can be served in pot lucks, game nights, and kitty parties. The crispy ladoos are also known as Murmura Ladoos and can be enjoyed both as a dessert and a snack.

Ingredients

  • 3 cup puffed rice
  • 1 1/2 cup cane sugar
  • 1/2 cup ghee

Procedure

  • Step-1 : To start with, take a saucepan and boil water in it. After the water boils, add jaggery (cane sugar) to the water and make a syrup.
  • Step-2 : Stir occasionally and remove from heat once done. Add kurmura (puffed rice), ghee to this syrup and make balls using this mixture. Serve warm. You can even store them.
