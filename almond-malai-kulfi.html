Almond Malai Kulfi

About Almond Malai Kulfi

Looking for a quick Summer recipe? Then try this easy Almond Malai Kulfi at home and impress your loved ones with your smart culinary skills. So, just roll up your sleeves and nail this simple delight and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 130 gm almonds
  • 2 handfuls pistachios
  • 300 ml condensed milk
  • 6 tablespoon fresh cream
  • 70 ml milk
  • 1 pinch saffron

Procedure

Step-1 : Grind the almonds
To prepare this delicious treat, start by taking a pan over medium flame and boil water in it. Then, add almonds to it and boil them for at least one minute. Now, quickly strain the water and wash the almonds with cold water. The skin of the almonds will start to come off, so peel them off completely. Set the almonds in a grinder and grind them well.
Step-2 : Heat the pan
Next, take another pan and heat it over medium flame. Add the pistachios to the pan and toast them by stirring and tossing them around. Then, take the toasted pistachios and crush them into small pieces.
Step-3 : Add condensed milk
Now, take a large bowl and add the ground almonds with condensed milk and cream in it. Blend them all together and then add the milk along with saffron in it. Blend it well again before adding the pistachios.
Step-4 :Pour in the molds and refrigerate
Take a matki or a kulfi mold and pour the mixture in it. Cover them with butter paper and set them in the freezer. Take them out when frozen and serve the chilled Almond Malai Kulfi!
