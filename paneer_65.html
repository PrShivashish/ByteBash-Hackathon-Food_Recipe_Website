Paneer 65

About Paneer 65

Yearning for a delicious paneer recipe, then this South Indian-style paneer recipe will surely entice you. Paneer 65 is a popular starter that can be enjoyed with chapati as well as rice. Prepared with the goodness of cottage cheese, this is a perfect treat for your taste buds. So, if you too feel like relishing this amazing South Indian recipe, you can follow and prepare this amazing dish at home to impress your loved ones. So, without further ado, just follow us through these simple steps to nail this delight.

Ingredients for Paneer 65 (3 serves)

  • 3/4 cup and 3 tablespoon paneer
  • 3 tablespoon rice flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 3/4 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon yoghurt (curd)
  • 1/3 cup and 2 teaspoon water
  • 3/4 onion
  • 1 and 1/2 green chilli
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon corn flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3/4 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 3/4 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 3/4 cup refined oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 3/4 stalk curry leaves
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon red chilli sauce
  • salt As required
  • 3/4 tablespoon spring onion leaves

Steps to make Paneer 65

Step-1 : Coat and fry the paneer
In a bowl, add cornflour and rice flour with 1 tsp of salt and 1/2 tsp of black pepper and red chilli powder, ginger-garlic paste, cumin powder, garam masala, 1 tsp of lemon juice. Now, add your paneer to this batter and coat it well. Deep fry the coated paneer in a kadhai and keep them aside.
Step-2 : Prepare the sauce
Heat some oil in a pan and add the cumin seeds to it. Now roast the garlic with the cumin seeds and add finely chopped onions along with chopped curry leaves. To this, add chilli sauce and tomato ketchup and salt. Mix it all well on high flame.
Step-3 : Combine and serve!
In the end, add some curd with 2 tbsp of water. Let it bubble and turn the flame to low and add the fried paneer pieces to this. Coat and combine well, top with some lemon juice, garnish with spring onion greens and serve hot!

Tips

  • Homemade paneer works best for this recipe. You can also use store-bought paneer. Just soak it in warm water for 15-20 minutes.
  • You can skip the food colour in this recipe or use Kashmiri red chilli powder.
  • Try to use fresh hung curd in the gravy for paneer 65.
