Mushroom Do Pyaza

About Mushroom Do Pyaza

Are you calorie-conscious and a food-lover? Then, you must try this super healthy and delicious Mushroom Do Pyaaza. This North Indian Dish is made using simple ingredients like button mushroom, lots of onion, tomato, green chillies, milk, bay leaf, garnished with fresh coriander leaves and a melange of aromatic spices. This low-carb dish recipe is an ideal lunch recipe for special occasions like kitty party, birthday, buffet and even game night. Prepare this spicy and delicious Mushroom Do Pyaaza for your near and dear ones and relish the authentic flavours with tandoor naan/roti or parantha.

Ingredients for Mushroom Do Pyaza (4 serves)

  • 250 gm diced button mushroom
  • 2 tablespoon onion paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 2 sliced & slit green chillies
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon powdered turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped coriander leaves
  • salt as required
  • 3 onion
  • 2 finely chopped tomato
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 5 tablespoon virgin olive oil

Steps to make Mushroom Do Pyaza

Step-1 : Heat and fry
Firstly, heat oil in a wok, add asafoetida, cumin seeds and bay leaf. When the seeds crackle, add 1 chopped onion and green chilies. Stir-fry until onions are light brown in colour, add ginger-garlic paste and saute for a minute.
Step-2 : Add onion paste followed by turmeric powder and other
Then, add onion paste followed by turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, curry powder and chilli powder. Mix the ingredients well with a spatula. Stir-fry until the raw smell of onion goes off.
Step-3 : Prepare the gravy
When the mixture starts leaving oil, add chopped tomatoes and sprinkle salt. Cook till tomatoes are tender and become mushy. Now, add 2 cubed onions and mushrooms, mix well and cover with a lid.
Step-4 : Add milk and stir
Cook over medium flame until onions become transparent. Now, add milk and boil it while stirring constantly to avoid cuddling of milk. Add some water according to your preference of gravy consistency.
Step-5 : Add garam masala powder and salt
Add garam masala powder and salt, mix well. Lastly, garnish with green coriander leaves and switch off the flame. Mix well and serve with roti/rice/parantha or naan.

Tips

  • Homemade mushroom works best for this recipe. You can also use store-bought paneer. Just soak it in warm water for 15-20 minutes.
  • You can skip the food colour in this recipe or use Kashmiri red chilli powder.
  • Try to use fresh gravy for mushroom masala.
