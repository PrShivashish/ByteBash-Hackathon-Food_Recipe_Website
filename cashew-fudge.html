Cashew Fudge

About Cashew Fudge

These traditional diamond-shaped Indian dessert is not only a go-to sweet for Diwali but is also the perfect dessert when you are craving something sweet. If you too love Kaju barfi as a sweet, make sure you try this super-easy Kaju fudge recipe which is made in a jiffy and also satisfies your taste buds. Made with easily available ingredients like kaju, khoya, sugar and ghee, this kaju fudge recipe is a must try. Why buy sweets from the store when you can make this delight at home and customize it according to your taste and preference. Follow the below given step-by-step recipe and make this delicious kaju fudge for yourself and your family.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup cashews
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered green cardamom
  • 500 gm khoya
  • 2 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 silver eating foil (for garnishing)

Procedure

  • Step-1 : Blend the cashews in a mixer grinder till they turn into a coarse powder.
  • Step-2 : Now sieve the powdered cashews to ensure that there is no lump.
  • Step-3 : Mix sugar and khoya in a separate bowl.
  • Step-4 : Transfer the sugar-khoya mixture into a pan. Add 1/2 cup water and stir for 5 minutes. Eventually, sugar gets dissolved and the mixture gets thickened.
  • Step-5 : Add in cardamom powder, ghee and the powdered cashews into it. Stir continuously to form a smooth paste.
  • Step-6 : Don't overcook if you want to keep the fudge softer.
  • Step-7 : Transfer the mixture into a big plate or tray. Roll out evenly using a rolling pin. Make sure that the fudge is thick enough while rolling it.
  • Step-8 : Now grease it using a teaspoon of ghee and apply edible silver foil on it. (This step is completely optional)
  • Step-9 : Cut into diamond shapes and your cashew fudge is ready to be served.
