Malai Laddoo

About Malai Laddoo

From boondi and besan to atta and motichoor, there are so many different versions of laddoo available to us. This toothsome dessert is a delight to have and is loved by people of all ages. Be it a festival or any auspicious occasion, laddoo is an essential part of celebrations. In this recipe, we will tell you how to make Malai Laddoos in the easiest way possible. You don't have to cook anything in this recipe and with just five ingredients you will get the tastiest laddoos. So, do try this recipe, rate it and let us know how it turned out to be.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup roasted semolina
  • 1/2 cup coconut powder
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Procedure

Step-1 : Roast the semolina
For this recipe, you need roasted semolina. If you don't have pre-roasted semolina or sooji, here is how to roast it. Keep a kadhai on medium flame and add semolina to it. Dry roast on a medium flame for a few minutes. Keep stirring it, till it turns golden. Remove from heat and let it cool down.
Step-2 : Prepare the mixture
Add roasted sooji, coconut powder, powdered sugar and fresh cream or malai to a big bowl. Use your hands to knead everything. If need, add some more malai. Knead well to prepare a thick mixture. Add raisins to it and mix well again.
Step-3 : Make laddoos
Now take out a small ball from the mixture and roll it between your palms to make a laddoo. Make more such laddoos from the mixture and lay them out in a tray.
Step-4 : Ready to be servede
Keep in the fridge for 15-20 minutes and your Malai Laddoos are ready to be served.
