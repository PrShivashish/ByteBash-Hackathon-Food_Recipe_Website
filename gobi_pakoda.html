Gobi Pakoda

About Gobi Pakoda

Craving for some delicious fritters? Then here's a perfect fix for your hunger pangs! Gobi Pakoda is a delicious snack recipe, which you can prepare for your loved ones anytime. This pakoda recipe is an easy-to-make dish, which is cooked with cauliflower, gram flour, and a few spices like dry mango powder, red chilli powder and salt. If you have some unexpected guests at your home, this is an apt dish to make along with a cup of hot tea or coffee. This lip-smacking fritter recipe can be made quickly anytime. Enjoy!

Ingredients for Gobi Pakoda (8 serves)

  • 200 gm cauliflower
  • 2 teaspoon dry mango powder
  • 2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • water as required
  • 1 dash baking soda
  • 2 cup refined oil
  • 1 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
  • salt as required
  • 1 dash turmeric

Steps to make Gobi Pakoda

Step-1 : Wash the Gobi
To begin this recipe, wash cauliflower under running water and break them into florets. Then take a pan and pour some water, and add the cauliflower florets with a dash of salt, partially boil the florets and drain the excess water. Keep the florets aside.
Step-2 : Make the batter
Take another bowl, add the gram flour (besan), salt, 1 teaspoon oil, turmeric, red chili powder, dry mango powder, baking soda, mix all the ingredients together and gradually add water. Keep stirring so that the batter doesn't form lumps. Do not add too much water as the batter should not be too thin or too thick.
Step-3 : Deep fry the pakoda
Put a kadhai on medium flame and add oil in it. When the oil is hot enough, dip each piece of the cauliflower in this batter and fry them in the kadhai, until they turn golden brown in colour. Take out the pakoda on a plate covered with tissue paper and serve hot with chutney and ketchup.

Tips

  • Make sure you don't over cook the florets of cauliflower else it will spoil the texture of the dish.
  • You can also add some ajwain to the batter, this prevents bloating.
  • You can also add some chopped coriander leaves to accentuate the taste.
