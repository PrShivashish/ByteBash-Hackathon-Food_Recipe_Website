Fat Burner Tea

What is Burner Tea

If you are looking to lose some extra inches, here's the easiest way to do it at home. All you need to do is just boil some cumin seeds and cinnamon sticks in water and make it a nice tea. This tea can be a lot to take in as the taste can be quite bitter. Thus, it is best enjoyed with some honey. Cumin seeds are known to be super-healthy and can help boost your metabolism levels, which can, in turn, promote weight loss. It also helps in lowering cholesterol levels.

Ingredients for Fat burner Tea

  • 3 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 litre water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey
Step-1 :Boil water with cumin and cinnamon
Take a large saucepan and put it over medium flame with 1 litre of water in it. Bring it to a boil and then add cumin seeds in it along with the cinnamon stick.
Step-2 :Boil the spices for 5 minutes
Let the spices boil for 5 minutes or more or until the flavours are absorbed. Once done, turn off the flame and let the water cool for some time.
Step-3 :Strain and mix honey
Afterwards, strain the drink into a tall glass and add 1 tsp honey to it. Mix it well and drink.
