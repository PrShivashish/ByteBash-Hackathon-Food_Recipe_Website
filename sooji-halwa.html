Sooji Halwa

About Sooji Halwa

Sooji Halwa is an easy-to-make dessert recipe that you can prepare to treat your loved ones anytime. If you are feeling like eating a sweet dish, then you must try this simple recipe which is prepared with semolina or sooji, sugar, ghee, cashews, condensed milk, almonds. You can make this easy recipe on special occasions and festivals. Try it and give your taste buds a delectable experience.

Ingredients

  • 500 gm semolina
  • 250 gm sugar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon green cardamom
  • 5 strands saffron
  • 200 gm ghee
  • 10 cashews
  • 10 almonds
  • 1 tablespoon condensed milk
  • water as required

Procedure

  • Step-1 : Take a boiler, add water in it and boil. When it starts boiling, add sugar along with saffron strands. Remember never allow this syrup to form any strands.
  • Step-2 : Take a pan, add ghee and roast almonds and cashew pieces and keep aside.
  • Step-3 : To that pan add more ghee, add sooji, fry well till it turns light brown and its well roasted and then add bay leaves. Add the boiling sugar syrup to it. Mix well.
  • Step-4 : Then, add condensed milk and roasted almonds and cashews. Garnish with green cardamom powder and mix well. Serve hot.
