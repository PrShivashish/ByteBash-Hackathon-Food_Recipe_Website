Kitkat Shake

About Kitkat Shake

Yearning for a thick, creamy chocolaty shake, but don't feel like ordering from outside! Well, we have got your back and we are sure this homemade KitKat shake will make you ditch store bought drinks. To make this creamy chocolaty shake you just need a few simple ingredints along with a pack of Kitkat and you can nail this restaurant-style shake in just a few minutes. Follow us through these simple steps and nail it like a pro!

Ingredients for Kitkat Shake

  • 2 kit kat
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 4 tablespoon chocolate sauce
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 600 ml milk
  • 1 teaspoon coffee
  • chocolate chips as required
  • 2 scoops chocolate icecream

Procedure

Step-1 : Blend KitKat and milk
To begin with this easy recipe, take a blender and add in milk, KitKat pieces, 1 tablespoon sugar,2 tablespoon chocolate syrup, blend it all together. Blend it again after adding 2 scoops of chocolate ice cream.
Step-2 : Prepare the whipped cream
Put the shake in a refrigerator for 15 mins. Next, take a large bowl and add whipped cream, coffee powder and sugar, whisk the whipped cream.
Step-3 :Serve chilled
Pour the cold shake into the serving glasses and drizzle the chocolate syrup. Next, using a piping cone add the whipped cream and sprinkle chocolate chips. Enjoy!

Tip

  • To make it healthy you can use soy milk and skip adding sugar.
