Mushroom Masala

About Mushroom Masala

Prepared in delicious tomato gravy, Mushroom Masala is an interesting curry made of mushrooms and a little bit of spices. This is an easy to make and spicy curry that you can enjoy with chapatis and even steamed rice. Try it!

Ingredients for Mushroom Masala (3 serves)

  • 350 gm sliced mushroom
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 2 small minced tomato
  • 1/2 inches cinnamon
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 2 powdered red chilli
  • 1/8 teaspoon powdered turmeric
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 small minced onion
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 3 soaked,ground cashews
  • 1 clove
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 4 pinches powdered salt
  • 5 tablespoon sunflower oil
  • 2 medium capsicum (green pepper)

Steps to make Mushroom Masala

Step-1 : Marinate the paneer pieces
For making this easy lunch/dinner recipe, dry roast the cinnamon, clove and cardamom in a pan, and grind them to a fine powder.
Step-2 : Bake the marinated paneer
Saute the onions until they turn pink and oil starts to separate. Add the tomatoes and capsicum. Stir-fry for a minute or so.
Step-3 : Prepare the gravy
After preparing the fine powder, in the next step, heat sunflower oil in a pan and add the minced onions along with ginger and garlic paste.
Step-4 : Add masala powder & let the gravy thicken
Now add coriander powder, red chilli powder and turmeric powder. Add the ground masala powder and cashews. Mix well.
Step-5 : Cooking
Cover and cook for 5-10 minutes.
Step-6 : Ready to serve
Mushroom masala is ready to be served. Just garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with chapati or rice of your choice.

Tips

  • Homemade mushroom works best for this recipe. You can also use store-bought mushroom. Just soak it in warm water for 15-20 minutes.
  • You can skip the food colour in this recipe or use Kashmiri red chilli powder.
  • Try to use fresh hung curd in the gravy for mushroom masala.
