Coconut Milkshake

What is Coconut Milkshake

A refreshing yet delicious milkshake that you can have in your breakfast along with some sandwiches, Coconut Milkshake is supremely healthy. It contains a significant amount of saturated fat which is essential for the body, but make sure that you never overdo this shale as it can lead to a sudden weight gain. This tropical delight is prepared using coconut milk, coconut cream, vanilla ice cream and some ice cubes.

Ingredients for Coconut Milkshake

  • 150 ml coconut milk
  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon chocolate powder
  • 40 ml coconut cream
  • 8 ice cubes
  • 1 tablespoon whipping cream

Procedure

  1. Blend all the ingredients in a blender jar, add all the ingredients and blend them all well together until frothy.
  2. Garnish with whipping cream and chocolate powder Pour the shake into a glass and top it up with some whipping cream with chocolate powder.
